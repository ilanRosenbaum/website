## Key
- Enjoyment: Enjoyment is a 1-10 rating of how much I enjoyed my specific trip to the place in question. Low numbers better. It may say very little about the place itself, sometimes I go on a great trip to a bad place or a bad trip to a great place. 
- Want to return: I feel like this is pretty self explanatory. Lower is better, these are comparative scores. Some places I will have had a wonderful trip but have no interest in coming back, some places it'll be the other way around.
